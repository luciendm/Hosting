Requirements: Members must meet rental agency rules:

  • Age requirement
  • Valid driver's license
  • Credit card in member's name
  • Deposit may be required

Exceptions & Special Cases:

  • Change rental agency? Contact agency directly.
  • No locations in RADAR? Expand search or call agency.
  • Non-ACE territory / Call Move? Provide servicing Club contact.

Multi-Day Rentals:

  • Premier: First day free, additional days follow normal terms.
  • Classic & Plus: All days are at discounted rate / upgrade rules.

Out-of-Territory / Call Move:

  • Cannot reserve via RADAR.
  • Provide servicing Club phone number or transfer call.

Reimbursement / Service Charges:

  • Premier members in service charge: Benefits default to Classic.
  • Free one-day rental only available if eligible and not previously used.
  • Reimbursement may be considered under specific conditions.
